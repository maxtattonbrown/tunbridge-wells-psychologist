Helping you live the life you want to lead

We are a team of HCPC-registered Clinical Psychologists who offer therapy for adults and children face-to-face and online. We tailor support to your needs using evidence based therapy approaches.

Providing specialised support tailored to your individual needs

Supporting both adults and children with both every day concerns through to more complex psychological difficulties. TWP offers high quality private Psychological Therapy both face-to-face in central Tunbridge Wells and online via video.

We have a team of highly experienced, HCPC-registered Clinical Psychologists who have the training and expertise to treat a range of psychological difficulties and mental health conditions, including:

  • Stress 

  • Low mood / depression

  • Anxiety

  • Panic Attacks

  • Phobias

  • Generalised Anxiety Disorder (GAD)

  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

  • Low self-esteem

  • Relationship difficulties 

  • Cancer

  • Work related stress

  • Public speaking anxiety

  • Traumatic life events

  • Adjusting to parenthood 

  • Post-natal anxiety and depression

  • Bereavement and loss

  • Living with a chronic / acute physical health condition

  • Sleep difficulties/insomnia

  • Emotional regulation/mood swings

  • Neurodiversity
